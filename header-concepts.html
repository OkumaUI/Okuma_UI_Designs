Header Redesign — 5 Concepts

A persistent, sticky global header for every page. Each concept surfaces the account scope (company, distributor, and the active machine) and puts a Browse Parts action right on the selected machine — so ordering the right parts for the right machine is always one click away. Click any machine selector to open its dropdown; the highlighted option is the active machine.

Option 1 Refined Classic — two-row context bar A polished evolution of today's header. White utility row (logo · search · icons · profile) over a blue context bar. The machine selector becomes a pill with an attached Browse Parts button — familiar to current users, low training cost.
